PROJECTS

League of Legends Stats ↗

2018 | ReactJS, Redux, HTML, CSS, MongoDB, Node.js, ExpressJS, Heroku

A journey of learning latest framework and tools like ReactJS, NodeJS etc. I have built a knowledge platform for League of Legends (League of Legends is a highly competitive PC game made by Riot Games). Where players can improve their skills by staying data-informed. This App offers the following:

  • › Player match history, including detailed information such as the history of all the items the player bought during every match. Death count, kill count, hero skill leveling order, etc.
  • › 'Most played with section' that shows how well the player did with their most played friends (top 5).
  • › Guides on how to play every single hero in the League of Legends game, including videos on hero skills, best items, strongest and weak opponents, etc.

Note: Riot’s APIs have changed drastically, therefore to use the app with legacy data that I stored before the API changes, please click the top-right search bar and search for Caenos.

Alternatively, feel free to use the Champions Tab to see all the guides and stats for each hero.
